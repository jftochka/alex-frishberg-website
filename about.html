About

Alex Frishberg was born in Kyiv, Ukraine, in 1963. In 1974, his family emigrated to St. Louis, Missouri. He received his J.D. from Washington University School of Law. Prior to relocating back to Ukraine in October 1991, Mr. Frishberg practiced at one of the largest law firms in Washington, D.C. His return to Kyiv marked the beginning of a prolific career as a chronicler of the post-Soviet transition.
